What our flat-pack assembly service covers

Flat-pack furniture is brilliant until it arrives in six heavy boxes and you've lost the Allen key. Sorn Handyman Services assembles flat-pack furniture of every brand across East Ayrshire — IKEA, Wayfair, B&Q, Argos, John Lewis, Dunelm, Made and Loaf. If it came in a box, we'll build it properly and fix it to the wall where needed.

Common flat-pack jobs

  • IKEA PAX wardrobes and sliding-door systems
  • Beds — divans, slatted frames, ottoman lift-up storage beds, bunks
  • Office desks, chairs, filing cabinets and home office suites
  • Dining tables, sideboards, TV units and bookcases
  • Children's furniture — cots, changing tables, mid-sleepers and high sleepers
  • Garden furniture, gazebos, sheds and playhouses
  • Full IKEA kitchen assembly and fitting (METOD carcasses)
  • Wall-fixing for safety — TV mounts, wardrobes, bookcases
  • Dismantling and reassembling furniture during a house move

Pricing guidance

Small items (a single bedside, a chair, a small bookcase) are £30–£50 each. Medium items like a double bed or a 3-door wardrobe are £60–£120. PAX wardrobes are typically £100–£180 per run depending on doors and internals. Full IKEA kitchen assembly is £300–£900 depending on number of units. Where several items are being built in one visit we discount the total — often cheaper than paying the retailer's own assembly fee.

Why book a local handyman for flat-pack

We bring a proper van-load of tools — battery drills, impact drivers, pocket hole jig, clamps, spirit levels, stud finder, the lot. That means we can do the things the instructions assume you'll handle yourself: adjusting doors that don't sit flush, finding studs to fix tall units safely, trimming plinths on uneven floors, and packing out units on out-of-plumb walls (common in older Ayrshire cottages). Every build gets wall-fixed where it should be — tall furniture should never just lean against a plaster wall where there are kids or pets in the house.

We'll also take all the packaging away and dispose of it at the local recycling, so your hallway isn't full of cardboard at the end.

Frequently asked questions

Can you collect the furniture from the shop first?

For most items around East Ayrshire, yes — we can collect from IKEA Braehead, B&Q Kilmarnock or Argos, for a small fuel charge. Just ask when booking.

Do you fix tall wardrobes to the wall?

Always. Every tall unit gets fixed into studs or with suitable plasterboard fixings — it's a safety issue we don't compromise on, especially with kids in the house.

What if parts are missing or damaged?

We'll build what we can, flag the missing parts clearly for you to report to the retailer, and come back at no extra cost to finish once the replacement arrives.

Can you fit an IKEA kitchen?

Yes. We assemble METOD carcasses, fit doors and drawers, install worktops, and can connect up the plumbing side too. Electrics we leave to a qualified sparky.

Do you take the packaging away?

Yes — we flat-pack the cardboard and take it to the local recycling centre as part of the job. Your hallway gets left clean.
