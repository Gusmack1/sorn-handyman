What our plumbing service covers

Sorn Handyman Services handles everyday domestic plumbing across East Ayrshire. We're not a gas-safe boiler company — for boiler installs and commissioning we'll put you in touch with a local Gas Safe engineer — but for almost everything else on the cold and hot water side, we've got it covered.

Typical plumbing jobs we get called out for

  • Replacing kitchen and bathroom taps, mixers and monobloc fittings
  • New toilets, cisterns, flush mechanisms and concealed frames
  • Radiator swaps, thermostatic valves (TRVs), bleeding and balancing systems
  • Full bathroom installations — bath, shower, basin, toilet and tiling
  • Shower enclosures, electric showers and thermostatic mixer showers
  • Fixing leaks under sinks, from traps, waste pipes and compression joints
  • Outside taps, garden hose bibs and washing machine connections
  • Isolation valves, stopcocks and service valves replaced or added
  • Burst-pipe repairs after cold snaps (common across Ayrshire in January)

Pricing guidance

A single tap swap, a washer replacement or a leaking trap is usually £60–£120 including small parts. A full toilet replacement is typically £180–£320 depending on the unit and whether concealed frames or new waste connections are involved. Radiator swaps (like-for-like) start around £90 per radiator. Full bathroom installs are priced job-by-job after a site visit — most in East Ayrshire land between £3,500 and £7,500 depending on spec. Written fixed quotes before any work starts.

Why a local plumber matters

East Ayrshire water runs soft, which is kind to boilers but can mean older copper is thin. Older cottages around Sorn and Catrine often have half-inch imperial pipework that needs specific adapters. Cumnock tenements have shared risers that need care. Being local means we've already worked on most of these and know what adapters to bring in the van.

Free quotes, no call-out fee within East Ayrshire, and a tidy finish — dust sheets, old fittings taken away, and no mess left behind.

Frequently asked questions

Are you Gas Safe registered?

We're not a gas engineer — for boilers, gas hobs and gas fires we'll recommend a trusted local Gas Safe registered engineer. Everything else on the water side we handle in-house.

Can you fit a bathroom from scratch?

Yes. We strip out, first-fix the plumbing, tile, fit sanitaryware and finish. Most East Ayrshire bathrooms take 5–10 working days depending on size.

What about emergency leaks?

Call Fraser on 07900 255876 — during normal hours we aim to be on site the same day for a genuine leak. Out of hours we'll point you at a 24/7 local emergency plumber if we can't get there.

Do you fit showers from screwfix or my own supply?

Yes — customer-supplied units are fine. We'll also advise on what works best for your pressure and cylinder type before you buy.

Do you cover Kilmarnock and Ayr?

Yes. We cover all of East Ayrshire — Sorn, Mauchline, Cumnock, Auchinleck, Kilmarnock, Galston — and can travel further for bigger jobs.
