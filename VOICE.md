# Sorn Handyman — Brand Voice

Two men, one van, East Ayrshire. Fraser (20+ years on the tools) and Aidan (qualified plumber / mechanical engineer). The voice on the page should sound like one of them leaning on the van door telling you what the job actually needs.

## 1. Voice Attributes

- **Grounded** — we name streets, postcodes, landmarks (Keir Hardie Hill, Glaisnock Street, Auld Brig). We sound like we've been there, because we have.
- **Specific** — 100×100 posts, MOT Type 1, fast-set concrete, £40 flat under the hour. Never a vague claim where a number will do.
- **Honest** — we say what we don't do (no gas, no full re-roofs, no scaffold jobs). We'd rather fix a gutter for £120 than sell a DPC for £2,000.
- **Dry / understated** — "reliably grumpy at 2am", "binned enough instruction booklets to paper a hall". Humour is a side-effect of being accurate, never a joke for its own sake.
- **First-person plural, personal** — "we", "us", "one of us — Fraser or Aidan". Named humans, not a brand.
- **Plain Scots-English cadence** — "Killie", "Ah-fleck", "Bonnet Toon", "the wee jobs". Local without being twee.

## 2. Must-Use Patterns

- Postcodes in every area reference (KA5, KA18, KA1, KA7).
- Real street names, landmarks, pubs, schools (Poosie Nansie's, Dean Castle, Barr Castle, Kames Institute).
- Prices with units: `£40 flat`, `£90/m²`, `£55/panel`, `from £120`.
- Materials by name: feather-edge, close-board, EPDM, Ubiflex, polymeric sand, Postfix.
- Timeframes: "within the hour", "same-day", "9am call, on site by 9.30".
- Em-dashes for the aside — the way a tradesman speaks — and Oxford commas left off where the rhythm wants it.
- Occasional named call-outs to Fraser or Aidan (who does what: Fraser = joinery/saw, Aidan = plumbing/WhatsApp/grumpy at 2am).
- Sign-off with the phone number: `Call 07900 255876`.

## 3. Forbidden Phrases

Never use: **solutions, premium, trusted, passionate, dive in, imagine, in today's world, one-stop, hassle-free, bespoke, tailored, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art, seamless, elevate, unlock, leverage, empower, synergy, holistic, game-changing, world-class, industry-leading, peace of mind, rest assured, look no further, your trusted partner, we pride ourselves, quality workmanship, customer satisfaction is our priority, 100% satisfied, 5-star service, free consultation**. No emojis in body copy (icons in the service list are the only exception). No exclamation marks.

## 4. Signature Rhythm

Short declarative, then a long specific. "Prep is 80% of the job. We sand, fill, caulk, prime and cut in by hand before any roller goes near a wall." One punch, then the evidence. End paragraphs on a short line where possible.

## 5. Pricing & Claim Rules

- No invented testimonials. No fake quotes from fake customers.
- No Trustpilot, Checkatrade, Which? or any review-platform claims we haven't earned.
- No "100% satisfied", "thousands of happy customers", star ratings, or review counts we haven't verified.
- Review count is low — that's fine. If asked, say so honestly: "we're a new-ish business in the village, reviews are coming in slowly."
- Insurance figure (£2m public liability) stated plainly. Certificate on request.
- Prices are "from" or "flat" or "POA" — never "starting as low as".

## 6. CTA Style

Primary: **Call 07900 255876** (day or night, one of us answers).
Secondary: **WhatsApp +44 7472 223323** (send a photo, we'll ballpark).
Never "book a consultation", "request a quote online", "fill in the form". The form exists; the phone is the point.

## 7. Exemplar Rewrites

**Generic:** *"We provide premium, bespoke handyman solutions tailored to your needs."*
**On-brand:** *Gutters, joinery, plumbing, fencing. If it's broken we fix it; if we can't, we know who can.*

**Generic:** *"Our trusted team is passionate about delivering quality workmanship and 100% customer satisfaction."*
**On-brand:** *Fraser's been on the tools 20 years. Aidan's a qualified plumber. We live in Sorn — the postie knows our van.*

**Generic:** *"Contact us today for a free, no-obligation quote and experience the difference!"*
**On-brand:** *WhatsApp a photo to +44 7472 223323. Ballpark back within the hour, firm quote same day. No call-out fee in East Ayrshire.*
