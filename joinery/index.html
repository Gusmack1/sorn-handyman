What our joinery work covers

We handle domestic joinery across Sorn, Mauchline, Cumnock, Kilmarnock and the wider East Ayrshire area. If it's wood and it goes in or on a house, odds are we've fitted it before. Our joinery service is aimed at homeowners and landlords who want a single local tradesman for everything from a wonky door to a full internal fit-out.

Common joinery jobs

  • Hanging and rehanging internal and external doors, including fire doors in flats and HMOs
  • Skirting, architrave, dado rails and picture rails — modern and period profiles
  • Staircase repairs, spindles, newels, handrails and full balustrade swaps
  • Built-in wardrobes, alcove shelving, under-stair storage, loft hatches
  • Kitchen carcasses, worktops, plinths, cornices
  • Window boards, cills and timber window repairs on sandstone properties
  • Decking, garden gates, timber fencing and sheds
  • Loft boarding, velux trims and attic ladders

Pricing guidance

Small joinery jobs (a single door hung, a loft hatch, one run of skirting) usually land in the £80–£180 bracket depending on materials. Half-day visits for a list of small tasks tend to be £120–£200. Full-day rates are fixed in advance after a free site visit so there are no surprises. Built-in storage, staircase work and decking are always quoted as a one-off fixed price once we've measured up. Materials are either supplied at cost plus a small margin, or you can buy them yourself.

Why pick a local tradesman

Being based in Sorn means we know East Ayrshire housing stock — the sandstone tenements in Kilmarnock, the 1930s semis in Mauchline, the ex-council terraces in Cumnock and the newer developments around Auchinleck. Traditional lath-and-plaster, old lime-bedded timber cills and modern drylined walls all need different fixings and different techniques. A local joiner who sees these every week fits right first time.

We turn up when we say, leave the site clean, and we're at the end of the phone afterwards if anything isn't right. Every quote is free.

Frequently asked questions

Do you supply materials, or do I buy them?

Either works. We'll quote with materials included at cost plus a small margin, or we'll give you a shopping list and you buy direct — whichever suits.

Can you match old sandstone window cills and frames?

Yes. We use hardwood or high-quality softwood with a microporous paint or stain system to match existing joinery on period properties across Kilmarnock and Mauchline.

How quickly can you start?

For small joinery jobs we can usually be on site within a few days. Bigger fit-outs (built-ins, staircases, decking) are typically booked 1–3 weeks ahead.

Do you handle fire doors for flats and HMOs?

Yes — we fit FD30 certified fire doors with the correct intumescent seals and closers, and we can provide photos and product certificates for your landlord records.

Do you cover areas outside Sorn?

We cover all of East Ayrshire — Mauchline, Cumnock, Auchinleck, Catrine, Galston, Kilmarnock and surrounding villages. Further afield is fine too if the job suits.
