What our painting and decorating service covers

Painting is one of the services we're busiest with. Sorn Handyman Services provides full interior and exterior painting and decorating across East Ayrshire — whole houses, single rooms, feature walls, exteriors, and landlord turnarounds. We do the prep properly, because that's where a paint job is made or broken.

Common painting and decorating jobs

  • Full interior repaints — walls, ceilings, skirting, architrave, doors
  • Single-room refreshes and feature walls
  • Wallpapering — pattern-matched, lining paper and hanging specialist papers
  • Exterior masonry and render painting with breathable masonry paint
  • Soffits, fascias and bargeboards (uPVC and timber)
  • Timber window and door repaints, including sash window refurb
  • Staircase and hallway repaints — sanding, knotting, filling, priming
  • Landlord refreshes between tenancies (tight turnaround, brilliant white standard)
  • Full prep: filling, sanding, caulking, stain-blocking and two-coat finishes

Pricing guidance

As a rough guide, a single bedroom (walls, ceiling, woodwork) is usually £250–£450 including paint. A full downstairs (hall, living room, kitchen walls) is typically £900–£1,600. Exterior render painting on a semi-detached is £1,200–£2,400 depending on height and prep. Wallpapering is priced per roll plus hang time. Every quote is fixed in writing after a site visit — no surprise day-rate add-ons.

Why choose a local painter

Ayrshire weather is what it is — any exterior paint job has to be planned around a dry window. Being local means we can jump on a good weather forecast and push a two-coat exterior finish through in the right conditions. On interiors, we know the typical quirks of East Ayrshire houses: old lath-and-plaster in Mauchline cottages, slightly dusty chimney breasts in Cumnock tenements, and the tough jobs of repainting over heavy wallpaper on the estates around Auchinleck.

We use Dulux Trade, Crown Trade and Johnstone's as standard, with Farrow & Ball or Little Greene supplied on request. All paint is trade-grade for coverage and durability.

Frequently asked questions

Do you supply the paint or do I buy it?

Either works. We usually supply trade paint and include it in the quote, but if you've got specific heritage colours we're happy to work from your tins.

How long does a typical room take?

A standard bedroom with walls, ceiling and woodwork is usually 1.5 to 2 days including prep, drying and two top coats. Bigger rooms and full houses are planned around drying times.

Can you repaint over woodchip or heavy wallpaper?

We can — but in most cases we'll recommend stripping the paper, lining the walls and finishing properly. It's more work upfront but the end result is night-and-day better.

Do you do exterior painting in winter?

Only if the weather window allows. Masonry paint needs surfaces above about 8°C and rain-free for several hours. We'd rather delay a week than rush it.

Do you cover landlord turnarounds?

Yes — quick, brilliant white refreshes between tenancies are one of our regular jobs across Cumnock and Kilmarnock. Fast turnaround, invoice for the landlord's records.
